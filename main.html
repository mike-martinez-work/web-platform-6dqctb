Welcome

Welcome

The United Independent School District has serviced 42,000 plus students for over 35 years with aquatic services. The new United ISD Aquatic Center will continue to provide valuable lifesaving and life skill aquatic services to our students in a state of the art facility.

Our aquatic philosophy adopts a total aquatic approach that extends into our programs and services, providing opportunities to “attain aquatic excellence for each and aquatic safety for All".

The United ISD Aquatic Center (A.C.) will maintain the infrastructure necessary to support the services offered throughout the year and will serve as an American Red Cross Provider. The United ISD A.C. will offer continuing education courses such as in-water safety, aquatic management, and instruction. Our focus will be to insure quality in the services the UISD A.C. offers including lifeguard training for students, staff development and the ability to service our aquatic community.

UISD's association with the University Interscholastic League (U.I.L.) will strive to continue to build a framework within the district’s swimming and diving community to develop a competitive aquatic program in South Texas.

This total program approach will service recreation and competitive swimmers as well as diving and water polo. The main focus will be aquatic safety, aquatic fitness, and the refinement of aquatic technique. The objective will be to provide a venue for our athletes to excel in the highest-levels of aquatic sports.

Mission Statement

Mission Statement

The UISD Natatorium shall provide an environment that will enrich the competitive opportunities of  student athletes and  access to community members.

Goal

Goal

The goal of the UISD Natatorium is to provide programs that will exemplify three aquatic components: 1. Educational Programs

  • Continuing and Re-certification Education-Courses
  • ARC Coaches Safety Training
  • ARC First Aid & CPR & AED
  • ARC Sport Safety Training
  • ARC CPR for the Professional Rescuer
  • ARC Lifeguard Training
  • ARC Lifeguard Management
  • USD Dive Safe Training
  • Profession Pool & Spa Operator Track (CPO/ AFO )

2. Recreational Programs (Tentative Developing)

  • Wellness Swimming (lap-swimming)
  • Kids Club (Summer Only)
  • UISD Summer Aquatic (stroke-school)(Summer Only)

3. Competitive Programs / University Interscholastic League (UIL)

  • UIL High School Swimming & Diving Program
  • UIL Junior Varsity School Swimming & Diving Program
